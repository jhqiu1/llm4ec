A curated collection of external resources related to **LLM-driven algorithm design and evolutionary computation**.

> ⚠️ **This page is continuously updated.** New links and resources are being added regularly.

## Surveys & Reviews

- [**A Systematic Survey on Large Language Models for Algorithm Design**](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.14716) — Fei Liu et al., *ACM Computing Surveys*, 2025. The definitive survey introducing the four-paradigm taxonomy (LLMaD/LLMaO/LLMaP/LLMaE).
- [**Evolutionary Computation in the Era of Large Language Model: Survey and Roadmap**](https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.10034) — arXiv, 2024.
- [**When Large Language Model Meets Optimization**](http://arxiv.org/abs/2405.10098) — arXiv, 2024.
- [**A Survey of Neural Code Intelligence: Paradigms, Advances and Beyond**](https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.14734) — arXiv, 2024.
- [**Leveraging Foundational Models for Black-Box Optimization**](https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.03547) *(Position Paper)* — ICML, 2024.

## Research Groups

- [**OPTIMA Group — City University of Hong Kong**](https://optima.cs.cityu.edu.hk/) — Multi-objective optimization, evolutionary computation, and LLM-driven algorithm design.
- [**Google DeepMind**](https://deepmind.google/) — FunSearch, OPRO, AlphaEvolve.
- [**LIACS — Leiden University**](https://liacs.leidenuniv.nl/) — LLaMEA, BLADE, explainable AI for optimization.
- [**Huawei Noah's Ark Lab**](https://noahlab.com.hk/) — EoH, ARS, LLM for supply chain optimization.
