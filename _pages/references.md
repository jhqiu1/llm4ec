## Paradigm Overview

| Paradigm | Acronym | Core Idea |
|----------|---------|-----------|
| **LLM as Designer** | LLMaD | LLMs autonomously generate, evolve, or search for novel algorithms, heuristics, and operators. |
| **LLM as Optimizer** | LLMaO | LLMs act directly as optimizers, iteratively refining candidate solutions via prompt-based reasoning. |
| **LLM as Predictor** | LLMaP | LLMs serve as surrogate models or performance predictors to guide the search process. |
| **LLM as Extractor** | LLMaE | LLMs extract structural features from problem descriptions to inform algorithm selection or configuration. |
| **Autonomous Research Agent** | — | End-to-end AI agents that autonomously conduct the full scientific research pipeline: hypothesis generation, experiment design, implementation, and paper writing. |
| **Benchmarks & Evaluation** | — | Datasets, benchmarks, and evaluation frameworks for measuring the capability of LLM-based algorithm discovery systems. |
