<p><strong>LLM4AAD</strong> — <strong>Large Language Models for Automated Algorithm Design</strong> — is an emerging research field at the intersection of <strong>Large Language Models (LLMs)</strong> and <strong>Automated Algorithm Design (AAD)</strong>.</p>

<p>Automated Algorithm Design seeks to automate the creation, configuration, and optimization of algorithms — tasks traditionally requiring deep human expertise. By leveraging the code generation, reasoning, and search capabilities of LLMs, researchers are now able to automate the design of heuristics, evolutionary operators, metaheuristics, and algorithm configurations in ways previously unattainable.</p>

<p>This site is <strong>continuously updated</strong> to track the latest developments in this rapidly evolving field. Contributions and suggestions are welcome.</p>
